Title: The Lost Kite
Level: Beginner

Sam had a red kite. He flew it in the park. The wind was strong that day.
Sam ran fast to make the kite go up. Up, up, up it went into the sky.

Then a big gust of wind pulled the string from Sam's hand. The kite flew
away over the trees. Sam felt sad. He looked and looked but could not
find it.

A girl named Mia saw the kite land in her yard. She picked it up and
looked at the name on it. It said "Sam" in blue letters. Mia walked to
the park to find Sam.

"Is this your kite?" Mia asked. Sam smiled a big smile. "Yes! Thank you!"
he said. Now Sam and Mia fly the kite together every weekend.

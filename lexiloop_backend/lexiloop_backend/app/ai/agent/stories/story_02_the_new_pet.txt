Title: The New Pet
Level: Beginner

Jack wanted a pet. He asked his mom every day. One Saturday, his mom
said yes. They went to the animal shelter together.

There were many animals at the shelter. Dogs barked. Cats meowed. Jack
looked at each one carefully. Then he saw a small brown rabbit sitting
quietly in the corner.

The rabbit had one floppy ear and one straight ear. Jack laughed and
said, "I want that one!" His mom agreed it was a good choice.

They named the rabbit Waffle. Waffle liked to eat carrots and hop around
the living room. Jack built him a little house out of a cardboard box.
Jack and Waffle became best friends.

Title: The Rainy Day Plan
Level: Intermediate

On Saturday morning, dark clouds covered the sky. Priya had planned to
ride her bike to the lake, but rain began to tap gently on her window.
She frowned and flopped onto her bed.

Her older brother, Arjun, knocked on her door. "Bored already?" he
asked, grinning. He held up a box of old puzzle pieces and a deck of
cards. "Rainy days aren't just for staring out windows, you know."

Together they spread the puzzle across the kitchen table. It showed a
picture of a lighthouse standing against crashing waves. Piece by piece,
the picture came together. By the time the rain stopped, they had
finished the whole puzzle and started a card game besides.

Priya realized that a rainy day could still turn into a good one, as
long as she found something new to try.
